Welcome to Saint John Donation Portal!

We have emailed, messaged, or called many charities in the Greater Saint John area and we are excited to announce that our full website, along with at least six charities and nonprofits, events, and social media, is planned to launch on June 1st.

Of course, as soon as we are able to gather charities, we will update our website with information necessary to help you make a difference in Saint John!

Meanwhile, you can follow our social media by clicking here for quicker updates and soon, events! We are planning to have monthly events to promote our caring charities, and I sincerely hope you all will participate in this easy-to-do event. Of course, no event is an event without prizes! Stay tuned for more information about events coming on June 3rd.

The website was created by students wanting to bring forth a welcoming change to the city of Saint John. For students, we grant opportunities to participate in a team, learning valuable teamwork and skills. For charities, we provide a one-stop portal for citizens wishing to bring a change in the city of Saint John. If you wish to participate in this initiative, please contact us!

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us by clicking the “contact us” button in our navigation bar.

Sincerely,


Daniel Kang, Founder and Lead Web Developer
