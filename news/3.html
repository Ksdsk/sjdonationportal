Featured Charities and Nonprofits of June
We’re excited to announce the featured charities of June!

As always, we want to thank the COVID-19 Frontliners behalf of all Greater Saint John citizens. Although cases in Zone 5 (Campbellton area) are showing development of COVID, we believe that working together, we can halt the snowball becoming an avalanche.

Horizon Health Network is a major guardian of NB defending us from the spreading of the Coronavirus. Please check out their webpage, their social media, and consider donating to the frontliners of Saint John.

Youth Enrichment Centres provide well-being to children, teenagers, and young adults in Greater Saint John. With schools closed due to the outbreak of COVID-19, it’s hard to take care of your children’ mental health and social life.
