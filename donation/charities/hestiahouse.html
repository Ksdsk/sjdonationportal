To provide a safe supportive environment for women and women with children who are victims of family violence, and to priovide opportunity for change. Hestia House is funded by the Province of New Brunswick (Department of Social Development) and receives needed funding from the United Way. It also relies heavily on the generosity and support of local citizens. The Sisters of Charity of the Immaculate Conception are our honorary patrons.
Hestia House offers:
  • Shelter for abused women and their children in a secure setting,
  • A 24-hour distress line that you can call and support or to discuss your option, and
  • Referrals to appropriate agencies and support groups for emotional, medical, legal, employment and housing needs.
