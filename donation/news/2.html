Saint John Donation Portal Launches!
Welcome to Saint John Donation Portal!

Saint John Donation Portal, a website designed to help donating to charities faster and easier for people of all ages, celebrates its launch with six charities. The project, started on May 2nd by students of Greater Saint John, is “A way to bring the city of Saint John closer together,” said Daniel, the lead web designer and co-founder of the project.

Bringing more online presence and attention to the charities of Saint John, connecting charities to people who support their passionate causes, and rekindling the spirit of the wonderful community of Saint John were the main intention of this initiative.

The current charities that will be taking part in this are:
  • Big Brothers and Big Sisters of Saint John
  • Teen Resource Centre
  • Horizon Health Network
  • The Salvation Army
  • Make-A-Wish Atlantic Provinces Canada
  • KV Oasis Youth Centre

SJ Donation Portal plans to add more charities as soon as we can, and is working every day to improve the website.

Coming every month are exciting events directed by the members and citizens of Saint John combined, promoting and applauding the caring charities and nonprofits of Saint John, as well as unveiling the hidden charities of Saint John. Starting with your participation, we can collectively make a huge difference and support the city we call home.

The website was created by students wanting to bring forth a welcoming change to the city of Saint John. For students, we grant opportunities to participate in a team, learning valuable teamwork and skills. For charities, we provide a one-stop portal for citizens wishing to bring a change in the city of Saint John. If you wish to participate in this initiative, please contact us!

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us by clicking the “contact us” button in our navigation bar.
