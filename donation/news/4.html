Our Vision for July
Welcome to Saint John Donation Portal!

It's amazing how much the web has developed. We currently have a custom donation webpage for every charties named in the list. We also have a new look from before! So please, when you have the chance, check it out and consider donating to the awesome charities!

Enough about the webpage, here are the four major updates we are planning to launch by August. Here is Jeremy Kwak, our marketer.

Hey folks! We apologize for our slow updates on our social media. We would like to give all of you an update about our organization and the future!

First off, a HUGE announcement! We soon plan to launch the Saint John Volunteer Portal, which will make volunteering easier for both organizations in need and the volunteers of all ages!

Second, we are still looking for charities of Greater Saint John to be stored into our database so that we can bring everyone closer together.

And last of all, we will be updating our social media more frequently to stay in touch with all of you. Thank you all for supporting us!

We hope to make Saint John better and re-spring our loved city back to life!

Sincerely,


Daniel Kang, Co-Founder and Lead Web Developer
