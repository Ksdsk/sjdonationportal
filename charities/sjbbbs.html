Big Brothers and Big Sisters of Saint John provides boys, girls, young men, and young women with a role model to talk to and share the experiences of growing up with. Through regular outings, a relationship is developed between the mentor and the mentee, which is built on trust and common interests, and is supported by our experienced case-workers. The result is a life-changing experience for both the mentor and the mentee. They also offer in-school mentoring services which provides youth with an adult or high-schooler role model and a friend to talk to and share the experiences of growing up within school grounds.
