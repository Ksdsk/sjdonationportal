Event of June
What is our event for the month of June?

We want to thank the frontliners of COVID-19 outbreak here in Saint John. Working hard to protect us from the virus, as well as taking an exceptional care for all patients, we would like to applaud to all medical workers of Saint John!

The image you see there is an American Sign Language symbolizing the word for 'Love'. For every post of you thanking the medical workers using the sign language for love and tagging the Saint John Donation Portal social media with it, you automatically will be entered for a chance to win one of $20 gift card of your choice.

Alternatively, you can also post the ASL sign for "Help" (As shown below) in honour of the people, the nonprofits, and the charities who have helped you.

This event is exclusive to those using Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram! We will DM the winners on July 1st, 2020.

Here is a copy-pastable text for your social media:

I am taking part in the #ThankYouSJ challenge by @sj_portal to give our frontline workers a huge thanks for their hard work to keep us safe! These gestures mean “love" and "help” in American Sign Language, and it is our way of showing gratitude for all the great work that was done by you. Thank you!
